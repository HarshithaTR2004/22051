Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For Gulab Jamun:
  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • Milk (as needed to knead the dough)
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sugar Syrup:

    2. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Bring it to a boil, and then simmer for a few minutes until the syrup slightly thickens. Add saffron strands and cardamom powder. Remove from heat and keep it warm.

  2. Prepare the Gulab Jamun Dough:

    3. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda. Add ghee and mix well. Gradually add milk and knead to form a soft and smooth dough.

  3. Shape and Fry the Gulab Jamun:

    4. Take small portions of the dough and shape them into small, smooth balls. Ensure there are no cracks.

    Heat oil or ghee in a pan for frying. The oil should be medium hot.

    Fry the shaped dough balls until they turn golden brown, then remove and drain on paper towels.

  4. Soak in Sugar Syrup:

    5. Place the fried Gulab Jamun in the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 2 hours to absorb the flavor and become soft.

  5. Serve Gulab Jamun:

    6. Garnish with additional saffron strands, if desired, and serve the Gulab Jamun warm or at room temperature.
