Chicken Momos Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Momos Dough:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • For the Chicken Filling:
  • 250g ground chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup carrot, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro (coriander leaves), chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Dipping Sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Momos Dough:

    2. In a bowl, combine all-purpose flour and a pinch of salt. Gradually add water and knead to make a smooth, elastic dough. Cover it and let it rest for about 30 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Chicken Filling:

    3. In a mixing bowl, combine ground chicken, chopped onion, minced garlic, grated ginger, chopped cabbage, grated carrot, chopped green chilies, cilantro, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Mix everything well.

  3. Shape the Momos:

    4. Divide the dough into small, equal portions. Roll each portion into a thin, round wrapper (similar to a tortilla). Add a spoonful of the chicken filling in the center of each wrapper.

    Fold the wrapper in half, forming a half-moon shape. Pinch and pleat the edges to seal the momos tightly.

  4. Steam the Momos:

    5. Place the momos in a greased steamer and steam for about 15-20 minutes, or until the momos are cooked and become translucent.

  5. Prepare the Dipping Sauce:

    6. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, and sesame oil to make the dipping sauce.

  6. Serve Chicken Momos:

    7. Serve the hot, steamed chicken momos with the dipping sauce. Enjoy!
