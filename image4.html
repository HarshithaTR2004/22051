Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (regular or parboiled)
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately and soak them in water for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds together into a smooth batter. Add water as needed while grinding.
  3. Transfer the batter to a large bowl and add salt. Mix well and let it ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight. The batter will rise and become slightly fluffy.
  4. If desired, add baking soda to the batter and mix well. This is optional but can make the dosas fluffier.
  5. Heat a non-stick or cast-iron skillet on medium heat. Grease it lightly with oil or ghee.
  6. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the skillet and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa. Cook until the edges start to lift and the bottom turns golden brown.
  7. Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it's golden and crisp.
  8. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
