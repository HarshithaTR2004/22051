Parotta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Water (as needed)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    2. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour and salt. Add vegetable oil and mix well. Gradually add water while kneading to form a soft and smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for about 20-30 minutes.

  2. Divide the Dough:

    3. After resting, divide the dough into equal-sized portions and roll them into small balls.

  3. Roll Out the Parotta:

    4. Take one dough ball and roll it out into a thin, flat disc on a clean surface. Brush the surface with ghee and sprinkle a little more flour over it.

    Now, start folding the disc into pleats, similar to a paper fan, until you have a long strip of pleated dough.

    Roll the pleated strip into a spiral, tucking the end underneath to secure it. This creates a layered parotta.

  4. Cook the Parotta:

    5. Heat a griddle or tava over medium heat. Place the rolled parotta on the griddle and cook until it turns golden brown on one side.

    Flip the parotta and cook the other side until it's also golden brown. Apply a little ghee while cooking if desired.

  5. Serve Parotta:

    6. Serve the hot, flaky parotta with your choice of side dishes, such as curry, chutney, or yogurt.
