Pongal Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup split yellow moong dal (lentils)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 10-12 cashews
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and lentils together and drain.
  2. In a large pot, combine the rice, lentils, and 4 cups of water. Add turmeric and asafoetida. Cook until both the rice and lentils are soft and well-cooked.
  3. In a separate pan, heat the ghee. Add cumin seeds, black pepper, grated ginger, and cashews. Saut√© until the cashews turn golden.
  4. Transfer this seasoning to the cooked rice and lentils. Mix well and add salt to taste.
  5. Simmer on low heat for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
