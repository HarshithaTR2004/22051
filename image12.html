Jalebi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tablespoon besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron strands (soaked in 2 tablespoons warm milk)
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sugar Syrup:

    2. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Bring it to a boil, and then simmer for a few minutes until the syrup slightly thickens. Add cardamom powder and remove from heat. Keep it warm.

  2. Prepare the Jalebi Batter:

    3. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, besan, yogurt, baking soda, sugar, and the saffron-soaked milk. Mix well to form a smooth batter. It should be of pouring consistency but not too thin.

  3. Fry the Jalebi:

    4. Heat oil or ghee in a pan for frying. Use a jalebi maker or a squeeze bottle to pour the batter into hot oil in spiral or circular shapes.

    Fry the jalebis until they turn golden and crispy. Remove them from the oil and immediately dip them in the warm sugar syrup for a few seconds.

  4. Serve Jalebi:

    5. Remove the jalebis from the sugar syrup and serve them warm. They can be garnished with chopped pistachios or almonds, if desired.
