Cheese Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pasta (penne, macaroni, or your choice)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta:

    2. Boil the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Cheese Sauce:

    3. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and cook for a few minutes, stirring continuously, until it turns light brown.

    Gradually add the milk, stirring to prevent lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens and comes to a gentle boil.

    Add the shredded cheddar cheese, salt, black pepper, paprika, and dried oregano (if using). Stir until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce is smooth.

  3. Combine Pasta and Cheese Sauce:

    4. Add the cooked pasta to the cheese sauce and stir to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.

  4. Serve Cheese Pasta:

    5. Serve the cheese pasta hot, garnished with additional shredded cheese and herbs, if desired.
