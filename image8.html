Vada Pav Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For Potato Filling:
  • 4-5 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • For Besan (Gram Flour) Batter:
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • For Assembling:
  • 6-8 pav (small white bread rolls)
  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Fried green chilies (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Potato Filling:

    2. In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.

    Add turmeric powder and red chili powder. Stir and add the mashed potatoes.

    Add salt to taste and mix well. Cook for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.

    Add chopped cilantro and remove from heat. Allow it to cool.

  2. Prepare the Besan Batter:

    3. In a bowl, combine gram flour, baking soda, red chili powder, and salt. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter. It should be thick enough to coat the back of a spoon.

  3. Make Potato Vadas:

    4. Take a portion of the potato mixture and shape it into a round, flat vada.

    Heat oil for deep frying in a pan. Dip the potato vadas in the besan batter and fry until they are golden brown and crispy.

  4. Assemble the Vada Pav:

    5. Slit the pav horizontally and apply green chutney and tamarind chutney inside.

    Place a hot potato vada inside the pav and serve with fried green chilies, if desired.
