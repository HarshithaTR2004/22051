Kothu Parotta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 parottas (leftover or freshly made), chopped into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.), finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • A handful of fresh cilantro (coriander leaves), chopped
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Parottas:

    2. Chop the parottas into small pieces and set them aside. If you have leftover parottas, they work perfectly for this dish.

  2. Sauté the Vegetables:

    3. Heat vegetable oil in a pan or griddle over medium heat. Add finely chopped onion, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until the onions turn translucent.

    Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes until they are tender.

  3. Add Spices:

    4. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt to the sautéed vegetables. Mix well to combine the spices.

  4. Add Chopped Parottas:

    5. Add the chopped parotta pieces to the pan. Stir well to combine and let the parottas absorb the flavors of the spices and vegetables.

  5. Sauté and Serve:

    6. Sauté everything together for a few more minutes until the parottas are heated through and have a slightly crispy texture.

  6. Garnish and Serve:

    7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with lemon wedges for an extra burst of flavor.
