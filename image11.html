Payasam (Kheer) Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup cashews and raisins
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Rice:

    2. Wash the rice and cook it until soft. You can pressure cook it with 2-3 whistles or cook it in a pan with water. Drain the water and keep the cooked rice aside.

  2. Prepare the Milk Base:

    3. In a large, heavy-bottomed pan, bring the milk to a boil. Stir it continuously to avoid sticking to the bottom of the pan.

  3. Add Cooked Rice:

    4. Once the milk begins to boil, add the cooked rice and continue to simmer on low heat. Stir occasionally.

  4. Add Sugar:

    5. When the rice is soft and the kheer thickens, add sugar and stir well. Continue to simmer and stir until the sugar dissolves.

  5. Fry Cashews and Raisins:

    6. In a separate pan, heat some ghee and fry cashews and raisins until they turn golden. Add them to the kheer.

  6. Add Cardamom and Saffron:

    7. Add cardamom powder and saffron strands (if using). Stir well.

  7. Simmer and Serve:

    8. Continue to simmer until the kheer reaches your desired consistency. It thickens as it cools, so adjust accordingly.

    Serve the payasam warm or chilled, garnished with more saffron and nuts, if desired.
