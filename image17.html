Fish Finger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 pieces of white fish fillets (e.g., cod or haddock)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Begin by preparing the fish fillets. Pat them dry with paper towels and season with salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  2. Set up a breading station with three shallow bowls. One with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with breadcrumbs.
  3. Dip each fish fillet into the flour, making sure it's coated evenly. Shake off any excess flour.
  4. Next, dip the fillet into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off.
  5. Finally, coat the fillet with breadcrumbs, pressing the breadcrumbs onto the fish to adhere. Repeat for all fillets.
  6. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Carefully add the breaded fish fillets and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the fish fingers from the skillet and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve hot with lemon wedges and your favorite dipping sauce.
