Vada Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 cup rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1/2 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the urad dal and rice together in water for 4-5 hours. Drain well.
  2. Grind the soaked urad dal, rice, and black peppercorns together into a smooth batter. Add water as needed while grinding.
  3. Transfer the batter to a mixing bowl and add grated ginger, chopped green chilies, cumin seeds, asafoetida, chopped cilantro, and salt. Mix everything well.
  4. Heat oil for deep frying in a pan or kadai.
  5. Take small portions of the mixture, shape them into round vadas, and carefully slide them into the hot oil.
  6. Fry the vadas until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove them from the oil using a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
